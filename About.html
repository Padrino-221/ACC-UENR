About ACC-UENR

Who are we

Welcome to AmaliTech Coding Club, University of Energy and Natural Resources! We are a passionate community of students dedicated to exploring the world of programming, software development, and technology. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder aiming to refine your skills, our club provides an inclusive space to collaborate, innovate, and grow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students by fostering a supportive environment for coding, problem-solving, and technological advancements. We strive to provide opportunities for skill development, networking, and hands-on experience through workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects.

What We Do

  • Workshops & Tutorials: Regular sessions covering programming languages, frameworks, and industry tools.
  • Hackathons & Coding Challenges: Competitive and collaborative coding events to sharpen problem-solving skills.
  • Collaborations: Work on real-world projects and build a strong portfolio.
  • Industry Talks: Engage with professionals and alumni from the tech industry.
  • Mentorship Programs: Guidance from experienced members to help you grow as a programmer.

Why Join Us?

  • Gain hands-on experience with coding and software development.
  • Connect with like-minded peers and industry professionals.
  • Participate in fun and challenging coding competitions.
  • Build an impressive portfolio and boost your resume.
  • Learn new technologies and stay updated with industry trends.

Get Involved!

We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're interested in web development, AI, cybersecurity, or competitive programming, there's a place for you here! Join us at our next meeting or connect with us online.
